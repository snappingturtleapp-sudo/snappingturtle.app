Damage Claim

Something arrive damaged?

If your photos arrived damaged due to mail carrier handling, we want to make it right. Please email us directly with the following information:

  • Your order number (begins with ST-)
  • Your name
  • A description of the damage
  • A photo of the damaged prints
customerservice@snappingturtle.app

We will respond within 1 business day. Reprints are issued at our sole discretion upon verification of damage.

Claims must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery confirmation. One claim per order. SnappingTurtle reserves the right to review all claims before issuing a reprint.
