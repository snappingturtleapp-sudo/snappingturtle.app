Introduction

SnappingTurtle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SnappingTurtle mobile application and website at snappingturtle.app.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Recipient mailing addresses that you manually enter into your address book
  • Personal messages you compose to accompany your photo sends
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe — we never see or store your card details
  • Email address if you sign up for our launch waitlist

Photographs

Photographs you select for sending are transmitted to Cloudinary (our temporary image storage provider) solely to fulfill your print order through Prodigi. Your photos are retained for a maximum of 15 days following confirmed delivery to allow for damage claim reprints. After this period, or after the 48-hour damage claim window closes with no claim submitted, your photos are permanently and automatically deleted from our servers. We do not use your photographs for any other purpose.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your photo mail orders through our print partner Prodigi
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To communicate with you about your orders
  • To send waitlist and launch communications if you have opted in
  • To improve and maintain the app

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We share your information only with:

  • Prodigi — our print and mail fulfillment partner. Prodigi receives recipient mailing addresses and photo files solely for the purpose of printing and mailing your order.
  • Stripe — our payment processor. We never receive or store your full card details.
  • Cloudinary — our temporary image storage provider. Images are automatically deleted after the retention period described above.
  • Mailchimp — our email marketing platform, used only if you opt in to our waitlist.

Data Storage and Security

Recipient addresses saved in your address book are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers except when required to fulfill an order. Your address book is protected by a user-defined PIN. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.
